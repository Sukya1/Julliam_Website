Learn about us

The Julliam program was founded by two high school students, Sukya Williams and Chloe Jules who had a vision of helping students in their community improve their math, reading and penmanship skills. The program has been expanded to a non-profit organization catering to the needs of students, parents and teachers. The organization now has over 400 member and we continue to grow our community across the US and Caribbean. We have a mission to meet the needs of every student we can. We have partnered with companies such as Walmart, Apple and Microsoft to make this possible.
